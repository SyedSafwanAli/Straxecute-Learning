Welcome to Building a Winning Career

Action Plan: Your Growth Opportunities

A lot of people end up succumbing to the mistake of setting goals but not making effective plans to reach them. But not you. That’s why you’re taking this program: you’re committed to building a winning career.

To do that, you need to identify not only the goals you want to achieve in the future, but the opportunities for development and growth that will help you over deliver in the present.

Your Growth

How will you need to grow to achieve the goals in your career? Use the five Ws as a starting point:

Who: Who can help you develop your abilities and achieve your goals? Always be thinking about expanding your network with mentors who can foster your growth.

What: What skills will better enable you to overdeliver in your current role?

Where: Where is your area of destiny? Are you developing yourself in ways that bring you closer to it?

When: Consider appropriate timing when seeking growth in your current function. When can you make contact with a mentor? When can you ask about taking on new projects? When can you take a course? Think about the future, too, but don’t lose sight of the present.

Why: As always, pursue growth with intention. Think about why you’re setting the development goals you’re setting. Always keep overdelivery and excellent performance in mind.

These Action Plan pages and the Game Pages in the Playbook will help you move from focused vision to forward momentum. You’ll continue to revisit your vision and goals regularly over the coming months and years, and they will naturally get adjusted. But launching a career without any vision, goals, or small steps forward isn’t a career launch at all – it’s just a job.
