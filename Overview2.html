It’s Your Career, What Are You Going to Do with It?

What It Means

Building a winning career is about pursuing a career that is personally and professionally fulfilling. This is a highly individual undertaking, and it is important to be honest with yourself as you assess your interests and skills and begin your journey.

Remember, you’re the one who is in control of your future. What you achieve is up to you and how you approach the opportunities, experiences, and challenges that you encounter.

Why It Matters

  • Because you spend too much time at work to settle for a job that doesn’t fulfill you. You deserve more than that – and you can get it if you are willing to try.
  • Because you have to take a proactive approach to building your career. You can’t count on others to manage it for you.
  • Because you need to have a goal in mind. Even though it might evolve and change as you grow, having something to work toward is a crucial element in building a winning career.

Course Content and Structure

This course consists of 10 modular sections, including a final “Test Yourself” section. The teachings and activities are grouped in a way that allows easy navigation and fluid movement between sections. You can also revisit any section at any time.

Each course section includes four components:

  1. Keynote by Jack Welch to introduce the section topic.
  2. Opportunity to review your present situation and think about the leadership challenges you face at work every day.
  3. Core content that dives into proven principles and practices for great leadership presented in an engaging format.
  4. Playbook to help you apply what you have learned to your job, making a direct, visible impact on your business's success.
