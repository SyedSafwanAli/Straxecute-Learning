Welcome to Building a Winning Career

It’s Your Career, What Are You Going to Do with It?

Some people naturally have a laser focus when it comes to their career. They know what they want and how they’re going to get there. Others… well, they might take a while to figure out where they want to go. While different people might have different starting lines, the goal of having a successful and rewarding career can be achieved by anyone who is willing to put in the hard work.

The first steps in this process are about understanding what led you to your current career position, envisioning how you’d like to grow, and beginning to build the skills you need to get there.

These Action Plan pages and the Game Pages in the Playbook will help you move from focused vision to forward momentum. You’ll continue to revisit your vision and goals regularly over the coming months and years, and they will naturally get adjusted. But launching a career without any vision, goals, or small steps forward isn’t a career launch at all – it’s just a job.
