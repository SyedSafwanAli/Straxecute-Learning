Overview

“Loving what you do, having fun at what you do – that’s the ideal. Find that intersection. Your AOD – your area of destiny – is where you’re better than somebody else… and you love doing it.”

Jack Welch

What It Means

Building a winning career is about pursuing a career that is personally and professionally fulfilling. This is a highly individual undertaking, and it is important to be honest with yourself as you assess your interests and skills and begin your journey.

Remember, you’re the one who is in control of your future. What you achieve is up to you and how you approach the opportunities, experiences, and challenges that you encounter.

Why It Matters

  1. Because you spend too much time at work to settle for a job that doesn’t fulfill you. You deserve more than that – and you can get it if you are willing to try.
  2. Because you have to take a proactive approach to building your career. You can’t count on others to manage it for you.
  3. Because you need to have a goal in mind. Even though it might evolve and change as you grow, having something to work toward is a crucial element in building a winning career.

Course Content and Structure

This course consists of 10 modular sections. A final “Test Yourself” section is also included. From start to finish, the teachings and activities have been grouped in a way that is easy to navigate and allows fluid movement from one section to the next. Plus, you can go back and repeat any sections at any time.

Each course section is made up of four components:

  1. Keynote by Jack Welch to introduce the section topic.
  2. Opportunity to review your present situation and think about the leadership challenges you face at work every day.
  3. Core content that dives into proven principles and practices for great leadership presented in an engaging format.
  4. Playbook to help you apply what you have learned to your job to make a direct, visible impact on the success of your business.

About the Playbook

A Mission-Critical Component The playbook is one of the most mission-critical components of a Welch Way course. It is a downloadable, printable document that is yours to keep and use. To ensure that all Welch Way programs are immediately applicable, we have focused on two core principles throughout the course:

Laser-focus on the practical You won’t find management “theory” in our courses. You won’t find complex formulae or convoluted calculations. You will get simple, to-the-point, real practices that have been proven to work in many of the world’s top organizations.

Learn it today; use it tomorrow The Welch Way Playbook is critical to ensuring that you can apply what you’ve learned. Each Playbook section is divided into three parts:

Takeaways

Providing a summary of key teachings from that section to remind you of what we covered and why it matters.

Action Plan

Built to help you analyze your situation, organize your thoughts, and develop concrete action steps to bring about change to help your company win. Each Action Plan section includes:

  1. Starting Point – thinking through your situation
  2. Quick Wins – simple ideas for fast action that take very little planning (and usually no money) to implement.
  3. Planning tools to support larger initiatives through:
    • Gathering information and pre-event planning
    • Event execution
    • Post-event follow-up
